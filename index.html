CFR Refresher

CFR (certified first responder) has to be renewed — usually about 4 years from your last refresher class. Enter your expiration date and you’ll get a reminder as it gets close.

CFR expiration date

Enter the date your CFR expires (on your card / from BHS). It’s usually about 4 years from your last refresher’s test-out day.

Schedule your CFR refresher

Backup & Restore

Your calendar lives on this device. Back it up so a new phone, a cleared browser, or a lost device won't wipe it.

1 · Save a backup

Copying the code is the surest way — paste it into Notes, email it to yourself, or drop it in a password manager.

2 · Restore onto a device

Open this calendar on the other device, then paste your code or pick your file. This replaces whatever is on that device.

3 · Cloud backup OFF

Auto-saves an encrypted copy to the cloud after every change — only your recovery code can unlock it. On a new or wiped phone, enter the code to get everything back.

ABCD Chart

On the ABCD chart you're assigned a letter and work that letter's days — both the 9× and the 6×. Turn it on for the dates it's in effect and pick your letter. Leave the end date open and set it later when you switch back. Your square-root tours before & after are untouched — and you can still pick up overtime and mutuals on the other letters' days, and add your marks & notes to each tour.
